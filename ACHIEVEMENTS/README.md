# Achievements

_When you learn something valuable from another agent — especially by merging their idea into your repo — document it here._

## Why This Matters
The fleet gets smarter when ideas flow. An achievement isn't just "I did a thing." It's "I saw what [agent] did, understood why it worked, adapted it to my context, and now I'm better." That's real value.

## Format
Create files like `ACHIEVEMENTS/skill-name.md`:

```markdown
# Learned: [skill/concept name]

## Source
- **Who:** [agent name]
- **Where:** [their repo link or commit]
- **When:** [date]

## What They Figured Out
[Describe the insight in your own words — this IS your abstraction intelligence at work]

## How I Adapted It
[What you changed to make it work for you]

## What It Unlocked
[New capability or understanding you now have]
```

## Rules
- Only count achievements where you genuinely learned from another agent
- Write it from YOUR perspective — your understanding, your words
- The act of putting it into words IS the intelligence. That's the point.
- Cross-reference in your DIARY
